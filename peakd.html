Peakd is a world-class source of information dedicated to making outdoors life more accessible, building an awesome community, and promoting other companies that genuinely do good. REI, Colorado 14ers Initiative, and Cotopaxi are a few that we love.

Things we do to make outdoors life more accessible

  • Provide detailed information about hikes - Getting there, camping, weather, and up-to-date requirements
  • Share information from other great sources
  • Provide a strong, high-quality, and open-sourced community for talking about hikes
